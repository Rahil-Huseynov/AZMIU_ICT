Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How secure is the platform?

Our platform employs industry-leading security measures including end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and compliance with international data protection standards to ensure your information remains secure.

Can the platform be customized for our institution?

Yes, our platform is highly customizable to meet the specific needs of your institution. From branding to feature sets, we work closely with you to tailor the system to your requirements.

What kind of support is available?

We provide comprehensive support including 24/7 technical assistance, regular training sessions, detailed documentation, and a dedicated account manager to ensure your success with our platform.

How does the platform handle system updates?

System updates are deployed automatically during low-usage periods to minimize disruption. All updates are thoroughly tested before deployment, and you'll receive advance notification of any significant changes.
